Our Mission

To inculcate in the student the fear of God as the first principle in life. To lay the foundation that shall enable each child to attain his/her highest potential in life - morally, intellectually and physically. To inspire in the mind of the child a high sense of discipline and the love for excellence. To encourage responsible decision making and inter-dependence of thought with particular emphasis on domestic, national and international issues; To inspire leadership qualities such as moral standards, truthfulness, determination, and integrity. To design and teach a school curriculum that prepares students for Common Entrance, BECE (JSCE), SSCE and NECO External Examinations.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational center where through academic excellence, physical and spiritual training, well-rounded students are guided to have a positive outlook of the future and impact their world positively.
